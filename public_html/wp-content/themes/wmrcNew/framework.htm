WMRC 2010 Exhibition

The WMRC 2010 Exhibition will be held on the Queen’s Birthday weekend 12th to 14th June 2010 at:

Brandon Park Community Centre
645 Ferntree Gully Road
Glen Waverley.

There will be layouts in 4 different scales, modelling Australian, USA, Japanese, European and UK prototypes.
